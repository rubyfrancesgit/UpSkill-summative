Learn the skills of your wildest dreams!

UpSkill connects you with the tools & talent to grow your skills in the areas you’re interested in. Start learning new skills today – hands on, in person.

How Upskill works

Step One

Start by exploring Skills or Volunteer experiences nearby. Apply filters like painting, chess, or conservation to find your fit. Save your favourites to a watchlist for later.

Step Two

Once you’ve found what you’re looking for, book in for a session. Make sure you’ve checked out your hosts bio, read the reviews, rules, and details on cancellation.

Step Three

It’s grow time! Connect with your host for pre-session tips & questions. Contact the UpSkill team for any additional support – we’re here to help.
